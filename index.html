About Us

QTP Creationz

QTP Creationz is a female owned company.Started by Thato Priscilla Mangena,it all began at home. I saw that there was need for people to embrace their true identities without feeling judged by anyone.


We sell uniquely handmade accessories and apparel. Our items are inspired by the different culture and tribes around our African continent. We believe that fashion is a way of expernssion and we want our customers to learn to embrace their inner African Royalty. We are all about preaching the gospel of remaining true to your self, just being the BEST THAT CAN BE and being your own kind of beautiful.
